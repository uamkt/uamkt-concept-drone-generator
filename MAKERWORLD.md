## Title
Concept Drone Generator — parametric quad/hexa frame (UAMKT Open Studio)

## Summary (one line)
A free concept drone frame from UAM Korea Tech's Concept Drone Generator — pick a mission, get a printable frame + BOM + flight-stack config.

## Description
This is a **concept multirotor frame** produced by the free **Concept Drone Generator** at 👉 **uamkt.com/build/generator**.

Enter a mission (bird monitoring, mapping/survey, recon/security, cargo) and a payload, and the generator sizes an airframe class, estimates all-up-weight and per-motor thrust for a 2:1 thrust-to-weight target, builds a reference BOM, recommends a flight stack (ArduPilot / PX4), and exports this printable frame.

Two reference frames are included:
- **Quad, 500 mm class** — general perception / monitoring builds
- **Hexa, 650 mm class** — heavier payload + endurance

Want the reasoning behind every choice? The open build methodology is at **uamkt.com/build**, and the **Dronology** e-book series is on Amazon (**uamkt.com/publications/books**).

⚠️ **This is a concept / education model.** Printed structural parts and propellers are experimental — use certified propellers and a validated airframe for actual flight. Operation requires national airworthiness and RF (KC) certification. Not legal or safety advice.

Made by **UAM Korea Tech** — low-altitude airspace solutions · uamkt.com

## Print settings (suggested)
- Material: PETG or PLA+ (PETG preferred for durability)
- Layer height: 0.20 mm
- Walls/perimeters: 4
- Infill: 40–60% (gyroid or grid)
- Supports: none required for the arms; optional for the center plate
- Orientation: flat, arms in the print plane
